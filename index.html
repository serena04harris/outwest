Explore Utah & Arizona

Embark on a breathtaking journey through the deserts and mountains of Utah and Arizona. From towering red rocks to hidden slot canyons, these iconic landscapes await your discovery!

Must See Attractions

Zion National Park

Hike through stunning canyons and climb the iconic Angel's Landing for panoramic views of Utah's rugged beauty.

Grand Canyon National Park

Discover one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, with awe-inspiring vistas and incredible hiking trails.

Arches National Park

Explore over 2,000 natural stone arches and enjoy stunning desert sunsets in this iconic park near Moab, Utah.

Getting Around Utah & Arizona

Car Rentals

Renting a car is the best way to explore these vast landscapes. Consider a 4x4 for rugged trails and backroads.

Shuttle Services

Many national parks, including Zion, offer shuttle services to popular trailheads, making it easy to navigate within the parks.

Local Tours

Guided tours provide an excellent way to access remote areas like slot canyons, Antelope Canyon, and Monument Valley.

Things to Know

Weather can vary greatly, so plan your gear accordingly. Always carry plenty of water and be aware of flash flood risks in slot canyons.

Local Climate & Best Times to Visit

Spring (March to May) and Fall (September to November) offer mild temperatures, perfect for hiking. Summers can be scorching, especially in desert regions, while winters can bring snow to higher elevations.

Important Information Before You Go

Park Passes

Purchase an America the Beautiful Pass for access to all national parks. Individual park passes are also available at entrances.

Safety Tips

Stay hydrated, wear sturdy footwear, and always check weather forecasts. Let someone know your plans if hiking in remote areas.

Permits

Some hikes, such as The Wave or Coyote Buttes, require permits. Research in advance to secure your spot.

Local Resources

Visitor centers in national parks offer maps, advice, and updates on trail conditions. Stop by before starting your adventure.
